- **Audio**: Mid band energy (250-2000 Hz) - where most musical content lives
- **Visual**: Modulates XZ plane rotation (forward-back tilt)
- **Why Elegant**: Mid frequencies carry melody and harmony. XZ rotation adds depth perception, making the geometry lean toward/away from you as the music swells.

**Elegance**: High frequencies add sparkle and detail
- **Audio**: High band energy (2000-8000 Hz) - cymbals, hi-hats, harmonics
- **Visual**: Modulates YZ plane rotation (vertical spin)
- **Why Elegant**: High frequencies are airy and ethereal. YZ rotation adds vertical spinning motion, like rising sparks or floating motes.

**Elegance**: Attack transients create dimensional shifts
- **Audio**: Number of transients per second (attack onsets)
- **Visual**: Modulates 4D XW plane rotation
- **Why Elegant**: Transients are sudden, percussive events. 4D rotation is the most "otherworldly" axis, perfect for representing discrete sonic events that puncture the 3D space.
- **Range**: 0 transients/sec ‚Üí stable, 10+ transients/sec ‚Üí rapid 4D wobbling

### 8. RMS ‚Üí YW & ZW Rotations (4D)
**Elegance**: Overall energy drives hyper-dimensional movement
- **Audio**: RMS amplitude (overall power)
- **Visual**: Modulates 4D YW and ZW plane rotations
- **Why Elegant**: These 4D axes are hardest to perceive, creating mysterious motion. Linking them to overall loudness makes the entire 4D object "breathe" with the music's dynamics.

### 9. Spectral Flux ‚Üí Morph Parameter
**Elegance**: Timbre change morphs geometry
- **Audio**: Spectral flux (rate of change in timbre)
- **Visual**: Geometry morph parameter (0-1)
- **Why Elegant**: When the sound changes character (filter sweep, wavetable morph), the visual form transforms smoothly. Static sounds keep static shapes, evolving sounds evolve the geometry.
- **Example**: Slow filter sweep ‚Üí slow geometric metamorphosis

### 10. Noise Content ‚Üí Chaos Parameter
**Elegance**: Noisy sound ‚Üí noisy visuals
- **Audio**: Harmonic-to-noise ratio (0=pure tone, 1=white noise)
- **Visual**: Vertex randomization (chaos amount)
- **Why Elegant**: Pure tones create perfect geometric forms. Noisy sounds (distortion, breath, vinyl crackle) randomize vertex positions, breaking the perfect symmetry. Audible chaos becomes visible chaos.

### 11. Polyphony ‚Üí Tessellation Density
**Elegance**: More notes ‚Üí more subdivisions
- **Audio**: Number of simultaneously active voices
- **Visual**: Subdivision level (3-10)
- **Why Elegant**: Single notes keep geometry simple. Chords increase geometric complexity, adding more faces/edges. Visual richness matches harmonic richness.
- **Range**: 1 voice ‚Üí 3 subdivisions, 8 voices ‚Üí 10 subdivisions

### 12. Stereo Width ‚Üí Layer Depth
**Elegance**: Wide stereo ‚Üí deep holographic layers
- **Audio**: L-R correlation (0=mono, 1=wide stereo)
- **Visual**: Holographic layer separation (0-5)
- **Why Elegant**: Mono signals feel "centered" - layers compress. Stereo signals feel "spacious" - layers expand apart, creating depth. Sonic space becomes visual space.

### 13. Mid Energy ‚Üí Projection Distance
**Elegance**: Reverberant sound pushes geometry away
- **Audio**: Mid energy (proxy for reverb amount)
- **Visual**: 4D‚Üí3D projection distance
- **Why Elegant**: Dry sounds feel close and intimate - geometry appears near. Reverberant sounds feel distant and ethereal - geometry recedes into 4D space, appearing smaller and more remote.

### 14. RMS ‚Üí Scale/Size
**Elegance**: Loud = large, soft = small
- **Audio**: RMS amplitude
- **Visual**: Overall scale (0.5-2.0)
- **Why Elegant**: The most direct mapping. Loud sounds make big visuals. Quiet sounds shrink the geometry. Creates a "breathing" effect during dynamics.

### 15. High Energy ‚Üí Vertex Brightness
**Elegance**: Bright sound ‚Üí bright vertices
- **Audio**: High-frequency energy (2-8 kHz)
- **Visual**: Vertex glow intensity (0-1)
- **Why Elegant**: High frequencies are perceived as "bright" in audio terms. Vertices light up when high-frequency content is present, making them twinkle like stars. Dull sounds dim the vertices.

### 16. Mid Energy ‚Üí Edge Thickness
**Elegance**: Body/presence ‚Üí visual weight
- **Audio**: Mid-frequency energy (250-2000 Hz)
- **Visual**: Edge line thickness (0.1-1.0)
- **Why Elegant**: Mid frequencies give sound "body" and "presence." Thicker edges make geometry feel more substantial and weighty. Thin sounds have thin edges, full sounds have thick edges.

### 17. Transient Density ‚Üí Particle Density
**Elegance**: Rhythmic attacks ‚Üí visual artifacts
- **Audio**: Transients per second
- **Visual**: Number of particle effects (0-1000)
- **Why Elegant**: Each percussive hit spawns particles. Busy rhythmic passages fill the scene with floating motes. Sparse passages keep it clean. Creates visual echoes of rhythmic events.

### 18. High Energy ‚Üí Warp Amount
**Elegance**: Filter resonance bends reality
- **Audio**: High-frequency energy (proxy for filter resonance)
- **Visual**: Geometric distortion/warping (0-1)
- **Why Elegant**: Resonant filter peaks emphasize specific frequencies, creating tonal "distortion." Visually, this warps the perfect geometry, bending edges and faces. Clean sounds maintain perfect forms, resonant sounds twist them.

### 19. Spectral Flux ‚Üí Shimmer Speed
**Elegance**: Timbre modulation ‚Üí vertex animation
- **Audio**: Rate of timbre change (proxy for vibrato/tremolo)
- **Visual**: Vertex animation speed (0-10 Hz)
- **Why Elegant**: When sound modulates (vibrato, chorus, tremolo), vertices shimmer faster. Static sounds have gentle, slow shimmers. Modulated sounds create rapid, organic pulsing.

## üåä THE FLOW OF ELEGANCE

### Perceptual Coherence
Every pairing is chosen so that **seeing matches hearing**. When you hear brightness, you see glow. When you hear rhythm, you see particles. The mappings honor human perception and synesthetic tendencies.

### Dimensional Hierarchy
- **3D Rotations (XY, XZ, YZ)**: Driven by frequency bands (bass, mid, high) - the most perceptible dimensions
- **4D Rotations (XW, YW, ZW)**: Driven by dynamics and transients - mysterious, otherworldly
- **Scale/Morph/Chaos**: Driven by spectral features - organic, evolving

### Complexity Scaling
Simple sounds create simple visuals. Complex sounds create complex visuals. The system scales elegantly from a single sine wave to a full orchestral arrangement.

### Hybrid Control Philosophy
**User sets the canvas**, audio paints on it. Base values provide artistic control, modulation adds life. At 0% depth, pure user control. At 100% depth, pure audio reactivity. The sweet spot is 30-60% - enough modulation to feel alive, not so much to lose control.
